# Fresh paint

You're looking at it — the site has a brand-new custom theme. I retired the old
off-the-shelf look and built one from the ground up to match my logo: dark, neon, and
a little bit cyberpunk.

Under the hood it's still Hugo, still built into a container and shipped to my k8s
cluster via GitOps — the redesign was all about the front end. Same content, new skin,
and a layout that's finally mine.

More content refreshes are rolling out alongside this, so poke around.
