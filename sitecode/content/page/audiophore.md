### About
Audiophore is a low-latency bridge, written in Rust, that takes output from Synesthesia
VJ software and pushes it out to the wider lighting ecosystem — Philips Hue, WLED,
Art-Net / E1.31 fixtures, Nanoleaf panels, Ether Dream laser DACs, and OSC passthrough
for anything else that speaks it.

The goal is to drive your entire lighting and laser rig from one place instead of
stitching together a pile of one-off integrations. It's still pre-launch, but the bones
are coming together — follow along at [audiophore.dev](https://audiophore.dev).
